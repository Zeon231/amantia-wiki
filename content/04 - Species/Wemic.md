# Wemic

![[04 - Species/Species Images/wemic.jpg]]

Part human, part lion — a Tabaxi centaur in the broadest sense, though the Wemic would consider that description reductive. Powerful, proud, and fiercely territorial, Wemic occupy the same niche in the open savanna that Centaurs occupy on the plains: apex presences who define the land they inhabit. They share the Pridelands with Bakassari humans and Leonin Tabaxi in an alliance built on mutual respect and occasional tension.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Humanoid (Bestial)
> **Speed:** 40 ft.
> **Powerful Build:** Count as one size larger for carrying capacity.
> **Natural Weapons:** Claws (1d6 slashing + Str modifier); bite (1d8 piercing + Str modifier). Proficient with both.
> **Pounce:** If you move at least 20 ft straight toward a creature before hitting with a claw attack, target makes DC (8 + prof + Str) Strength save or is knocked prone. If prone, make a bonus action bite attack.
> **Darkvision:** 60 ft.

---

## Appearance

The upper body of a powerfully built humanoid fused at the waist to the body of a lion. Males often carry impressive manes — dark in dominant males. Coat colour ranges from tawny gold to deep brown; some carry subtle markings. Imposing at any angle.

---

## Found In

The **Pridelands of Nobanion** — the primary Wemic homeland, shared with Bakassari humans and Leonin Tabaxi in a complex social arrangement centred on the Lord of Lions.

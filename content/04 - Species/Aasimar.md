# Aasimar

![[04 - Species/Species Images/aasimar.jpg]]

Born with a divine spark from angels or other celestial beings, Aasimar are touched by the heavens from birth. Beautiful and slightly otherworldly, they carry radiant power that can manifest in moments of great need — whether as healing light, consuming flame, or shrouding shadow.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Humanoid (Celestial)
> **Healing Hands:** Touch to heal HP equal to your level once per long rest.
> **Light Bearer:** Know the *Light* cantrip.
> **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
> **Celestial Resistance:** Resistance to necrotic and radiant damage.
> **Transformation (choose one):** Radiant Soul (fly speed + radiant damage); Necrotic Shroud (frighten creatures + necrotic damage); Radiant Consumption (radiate blinding light + radiant damage). Once per long rest.

---

## Appearance

Faint luminescence; eyes that glow under stress; radiant features. Beautiful in a way that unsettles — too perfect, too bright.

---

## Subtypes

**Standard Aasimar** — Divine spark from any celestial source.

**Fae Aasimar** — Celestial heritage filtered through the Fae. Found specifically in the High Kingdom of the Green Stars and the Prydain Highlands. Their radiance has an eerie, moonlit quality.

---

## Found In

Throughout Amantia, particularly in areas tied to the active divine pantheon — the Holy Rose Empire and the Republic of Arcadia.

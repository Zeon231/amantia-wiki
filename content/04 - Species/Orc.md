# Orc

![[04 - Species/Species Images/orc.jpg]]

Tough, powerful, and built for endurance under brutal conditions. Orcs have a reputation that precedes them — usually earned by others, usually unfair to the individual. They are fierce when threatened and fierce when not threatened, but that fierceness is not cruelty. Orcs value strength as a tool, not an end. Those who've earned their trust find allies who do not yield.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Humanoid
> **Powerful Build:** Count as one size larger for carrying capacity.
> **Adrenaline Rush:** As a bonus action, take the Dash action and gain temporary HP equal to your proficiency bonus. Can use a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.
> **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
> **Relentless Endurance:** When reduced to 0 HP but not killed outright, drop to 1 HP instead. Once per long rest.

---

## Appearance

Larger than average humans — broad-shouldered, heavily muscled, with slightly prominent lower canines (tusks, though rarely the dramatic horns of popular imagination). Green-grey to brown-grey skin; dark eyes; usually dark hair worn practical rather than ornamental. Orc faces are blunt and direct, which people often mistake for aggression.

---

## Found In

Widespread frontier presence. Found throughout the northern kingdoms, the Untamed Marches, and border regions throughout Amantia. Many Orcs serve in frontier militaries or as independent warriors; others maintain their own clan communities in less-settled lands.

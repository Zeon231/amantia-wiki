# Centaur

![[04 - Species/Species Images/centaur.jpg]]

Proud beings of the open plain — the upper body of a humanoid fused to the lower body of a horse. Centaurs are creatures of motion; they think best at full gallop and feel the pull of open horizons as a physical need. Their society prizes martial skill, speed, and the freedom of wide-open spaces.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Fae (Humanoid/Beast hybrid)
> **Speed:** 40 ft.
> **Charge:** After moving at least 30 ft straight toward a target, make a bonus action melee attack.
> **Equine Build:** Count as Large for carrying capacity; difficult terrain made of mud, shallow water, or similar costs no extra movement.
> **Natural Affinity:** Proficiency in two skills chosen from: Nature, Animal Handling, Survival, Perception.

---

## Appearance

Powerfully built — the human torso sits atop a horse's body at the withers. Coat and hair colouring vary widely; some carry elaborate braided manes. Typically imposing in size and bearing.

---

## Found In

Open plains and frontier regions. Found in the Untamed Marches and Sikassan grasslands. Centaur war-bands are a significant military presence in the open-plains territories.

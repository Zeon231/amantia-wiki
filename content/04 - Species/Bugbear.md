# Bugbear

![[04 - Species/Species Images/bugbear.jpg]]

The apex predators of goblinoid-kind — large, hairy, and terrifyingly stealthy for their size. Where Goblins scatter and Hobgoblins march in formation, Bugbears stalk. They are patient hunters who strike without warning and vanish back into the dark.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Humanoid (Goblinoid)
> **Powerful Build:** Count as one size larger for carrying capacity.
> **Long-Limbed:** +5 ft reach on melee attacks on your turn.
> **Sneaky:** Proficiency in Stealth.
> **Surprise Attack:** On the first round of combat, if you hit a surprised creature, deal an extra 2d6 damage.

---

## Appearance

Massive, heavyset goblinoids covered in thick, coarse fur. Broad, flat faces; small, beady eyes; ears that are large and bat-like. Their size (typically 7+ feet) is at odds with their extraordinary stealth.

---

## Found In

Wilderness regions, goblinoid territories, and frontier lands throughout Amantia. Often encountered as leaders or enforcers within Goblin communities.

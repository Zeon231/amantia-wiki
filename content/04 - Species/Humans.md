# Humans

Humans are the most common people in Amantia, found on every continent and in virtually every nation. Six distinct ethnicities exist, each shaped by geography, culture, and history. Humans are versatile, ambitious, and adaptable — qualities that have made them dominant across the world.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Size:** Medium | **Speed:** 30 ft | **Age:** Mature ~18, live ~80 years
> **Alignment:** Varies widely by culture
> **Languages:** Varies by ethnicity (see below)

---

## The Six Human Ethnicities

### Siórtharan
*Old Name: Ananthean*

![[04 - Species/Species Images/siorthara-human.jpg]]

**Inspiration:** Celtic / Irish / Gaelic
**Language:** Ananthean
**Homeland:** The Rose Subcontinent — the Siórtharan kingdoms (High Kingdom of Séraevirhae, Tir Aelyrhae, Tir Maelwynnara, etc.)
**Character:** The most numerous ethnic group in the western world. Deeply tied to druidic and bardic traditions, clan structures, and the natural world. Their political culture revolves around bards, druids, and hereditary monarchy.

**Physical Traits:** Pale to olive skin; hair ranging from red and auburn to dark brown and black; often freckled; green, blue, or grey eyes common.

**Cultural Notes:**
- Strong bardic tradition — the High Council of Bards and Druids wields real political power in many Siórtharan kingdoms.
- The Oath of the Wolf (Tir Maelwynnara) is an example of the deep covenant culture — binding mortals, druids, and spirits.
- Names tend toward melodic Celtic constructions (see [[Amantia Source Material/AppendixA_Names_09March2024.pdf]]).

---

### Iljarnskarn
*Old Name: Aislandtic / Il'tuik*

![[04 - Species/Species Images/iljarnskarn-human.jpg]]

**Inspiration:** Norse / Germanic / Viking
**Language:** Aislandtic
**Homeland:** Northern kingdoms — Stornhjem, Fjarrkaldra, Thulraskar, Elarathir, the northern marches of the Holy Rose Empire
**Character:** Hardy, martial, proud. Society built around clan chiefs, jarls, sea-lords, and strength. Rule is often held by strength and consent of chieftains rather than hereditary right alone.

**Physical Traits:** Fair to ruddy complexion; light to dark blond, red, or brown hair; blue, green, or grey eyes; often tall and broad-shouldered.

**Cultural Notes:**
- Revere ancestor spirits, rune magic, and gods of war and winter.
- Found in large numbers throughout the Holy Rose Empire as settlers and soldiers.
- Sea-faring tradition; longships and coastal raiding are part of their history.

---

### Malkûn
*Old Name: Al' Malik*

![[04 - Species/Species Images/malkun-human.jpg]]

**Inspiration:** Arabic / Middle Eastern / North African
**Language:** Al' Malik
**Homeland:** The Sultanate of Dār al-Ḥikmûn, the Ben-Hazzir Coast, parts of the Jewel Coast
**Character:** Scholarly, mercantile, and deeply spiritual. The Sultanate of the House of Great Wisdom reflects their cultural emphasis on learning, philosophy, and faith.

**Physical Traits:** Warm brown to dark brown skin; dark hair (black, dark brown); dark eyes; often lean and sharp-featured.

**Cultural Notes:**
- Strong merchant tradition; the Jewel Coast and trade routes are heavily Malkûn.
- Deep ties to the elder and primordial pantheons in some regions.
- Often multilingual due to trade culture.

---

### Bakassari
*Old Name: Sikassan*

![[04 - Species/Species Images/bakassari-human.jpg]]

**Inspiration:** West African / Sub-Saharan African
**Language:** Sikassan
**Homeland:** The Jewel Coast, Kaduna, the Great Shining Plain, the Pridelands of Nobanion, and southern Nesian regions
**Character:** Communal, spiritually rich, and deeply connected to the land and its spirits. Warrior traditions and griotic (oral history) traditions are both highly valued.

**Physical Traits:** Deep brown to dark brown skin; black hair in a wide variety of styles; dark brown or black eyes; often tall and athletic.

**Cultural Notes:**
- The Pridelands of Nobanion are a primarily Bakassari/Wemic shared territory with a strong lion-veneration tradition.
- Strong spiritual traditions around ancestor veneration and nature spirits.
- Leonin and Leopard Tabaxi frequently share regions with Bakassari communities.

---

### Vanakuti
*Old Name: Nesian*

![[04 - Species/Species Images/vanakuti-human.jpg]]

**Inspiration:** South Asian / Indian / Southeast Asian
**Language:** Nesian
**Homeland:** The Nations of the Spirit People, the Shessian Empire, parts of the Nesian subcontinent; Jaipur
**Character:** Philosophical, spiritual, and artistically sophisticated. Strong traditions of ritual, caste-like social structures in some regions, and spiritual devotion.

**Physical Traits:** Warm brown to dark brown skin; black to very dark brown hair; dark eyes; wide range of builds.

**Cultural Notes:**
- The Shessian Empire features both Vanakuti humans and Yuan-ti, suggesting a complex intertwining of serpentine and human culture.
- The Nations of the Spirit People have strong animist traditions.
- Rakshasa and tiger Tabaxi share territory with Vanakuti peoples in some regions.

---

### Arcavéan
*Old Name: Arcadian*

![[04 - Species/Species Images/arcavean-human.jpg]]

**Inspiration:** Mediterranean / Greek / Roman / Eastern European
**Language:** Arcadian
**Homeland:** The Republic of Arcadia, the Avignon Empire, Le Provence, Lombardi League; also heavily present in the Holy Rose Empire
**Character:** Civic, mercantile, and deeply tied to classical ideals of law, republic, and philosophy. The Republic of Arcadia is the heartland of Arcavéan civilization.

**Physical Traits:** Olive to light brown skin; dark hair; dark or hazel eyes; wide range of builds.

**Cultural Notes:**
- The Arcadian calendar days and gods form the template for much of the world (Aurinvaldrsday, Tirealsday, etc.).
- Arcadia is cosmopolitan — aasimar, genasi, gith, and greater minotaur are all found there.
- The Holy Rose Empire draws heavily from Arcavéan cultural aesthetics.

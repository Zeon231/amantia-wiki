# Bullywug

![[04 - Species/Species Images/bullywug.jpg]]

Humanoid frog-folk of the bogs and wetlands. Bullywugs are territorial, proudly hierarchical within their own communities, and deeply uncomfortable away from the wet places they call home. Outside their swamps they are curiosities; within them, they are formidable.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Humanoid (Bestial)
> **Swim Speed:** 30 ft.
> **Amphibious:** Breathe air and water.
> **Standing Leap:** Triple normal jump distances (long jump = 3× Str score ft; high jump = 3× Str mod ft).
> **Swamp Camouflage:** Advantage on Stealth checks in swampy or boggy terrain.

---

## Appearance

Bulging eyes on a wide, flat head; mottled green-brown skin that stays perpetually moist; wide mouths capable of enormous grins or threatening croaks. Hands and feet are webbed. Typically shorter than humans but powerfully built through the legs.

---

## Found In

Wetlands, swamps, and river delta regions. Found in the County of Mornolace's extensive bog regions.

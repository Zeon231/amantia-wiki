# Triton

![[04 - Species/Species Images/triton.jpg]]

Guardians of the deep ocean — aquatic humanoids who have spent millennia holding the line against horrors that surface-dwellers don't even know exist. Tritons carry themselves with the quiet pride of a people who have done important, thankless work for a very long time. When they emerge to the surface world, they often find it chaotic, ungrateful, and profoundly unserious.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Humanoid (Aquatic)
> **Swim Speed:** 30 ft.
> **Amphibious:** Breathe air and water.
> **Cold Resistance:** Resistance to cold damage.
> **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
> **Innate Magic:** Cast *Fog Cloud*, *Gust of Wind*, and *Wall of Water* once each per long rest. Charisma is the spellcasting ability.
> **Guardian of the Depths:** Adapted to high pressure; immune to the effects of deep water pressure.

---

## Appearance

Humanoid with aquatic features — scaled skin in blues, greens, and silvers; fin-like ears; webbed hands and feet; eyes that are large and adapted to low-light water. Some bear small dorsal ridges or fin-crests. They tend toward lean, athletic builds suited to swimming. Surface-appropriate clothing always looks slightly wrong on them.

---

## Found In

Coastal regions and deep ocean territories. Found near the Admiralty of Sjóskjaldheim and the deep coasts of the Jewel Coast. Triton communities rarely venture far inland.

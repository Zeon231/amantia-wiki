# Cervitaur

![[04 - Species/Species Images/cervitaur.jpg]]

Where Centaurs claim the open plain, Cervitaurs belong to the deep forest. The upper body of a humanoid fused to the lower body of a deer — more graceful than their horse-kin, built for swift movement through dense woodland rather than open-ground charges. Their Fae heritage runs close to the surface, and they are deeply attuned to forest magic and the spirits of the wild.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Fae (Humanoid/Beast hybrid)
> **Speed:** 35 ft.
> **Fae Ancestry:** Advantage on saves vs. charm; immune to magical sleep.
> **Forest Movement:** Difficult terrain in natural environments (forest, undergrowth, brush) does not cost extra movement.
> **Natural Grace:** Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks in forested or natural terrain.

---

## Appearance

Slender and elegant compared to Centaurs — deer legs are built for silent movement and bursts of speed, not heavy charges. Many males carry antlers, which vary enormously by individual; some have elaborate multi-tined racks, others small velvet nubs. Coat typically matches forest colours — fawn, dappled brown, grey.

---

## Found In

Forest regions, almost exclusively in Fae-touched areas of the Siórtharan kingdoms. Rarely encountered far from old-growth woodland.

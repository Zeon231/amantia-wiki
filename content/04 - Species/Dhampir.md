# Dhampir

![[04 - Species/Species Images/dhampir.jpg]]

Born of mortal and vampire heritage — or transformed by vampiric feeding that didn't fully kill. Dhampir are half in the world of the living, half drawn toward blood. They hunger without fully needing; they age without dying easily. They are not monsters, but they carry something monstrous in them, and most people sense it.

> [!info] Racial Traits
> **Creature Type:** Humanoid (Lineage)
> **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
> **Spider Climb:** Climb speed equal to walk speed, including upside-down on ceilings.
> **Vampiric Bite:** Fangs are a natural weapon (1d4 piercing). On a hit, you deal damage and regain HP equal to the piercing damage dealt. Once per turn.
> **Undead Resilience:** Advantage on saves vs. disease and vs. being frightened.

---

## Appearance

Human-like but slightly off — skin paler than it should be, eyes that catch light like a cat's, canines that are just a little too sharp. Dhampir run cold to the touch. Their beauty, if they have it, tends toward the unsettling — the kind of face you find yourself staring at before you realize why you're uncomfortable.

---

## Found In

The Duchy of Saxolace (deeply entangled with necromantic powers and vampiric noble families) and the County of Mornolace. Rare elsewhere; a Dhampir far from these regions typically has a story about why.

# Tir Maelwynnara

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** The Realm of Blessed Mists
> **Former Name:** Gallia
> **Ruler:** Rígan Siofra na Morvanyr
> **Government:** Hereditary monarchy bound by the Oath of the Wolf — an ancient covenant between mortals, druids, and the spirits of the fallen
> **Population:** 359,100

## Racial Composition
78% Siorthara, 7% Iljarnskarn,  5% Faenari Elves, 5% Gnomes, 5% Other

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Iron, coal, silver, cured furs, mead, amber, and spirits (particularly the strong liquor “Wolf’s Breath”)

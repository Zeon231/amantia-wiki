# Rosensk Morka

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** The Electorate of the Rose
> **Former Name:** The Palatinate
> **Government:** Feudal electorate ruled by the High Elector-Prince of Rosen, seat of the Parliament of the Petal Crown, which elects the Emperor/Empress of the Holy Rose Empire
> **Population:** 497,000

## Racial Composition
~40% Iljarnskarn Humans, 40% Siortharan Humans, 10% Gnomes and dwarves, 5% Fanari Elves, 5% other cosmopolitan lineages

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Fine textiles, silver filigree, ship fittings, relic replicas, and law

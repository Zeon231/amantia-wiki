# County of Mornolace

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** The Fief of the Dark Lakes
> **Former Name:** Gorislav
> **Ruler:** Count Aedric Fenwyck
> **Government:** Hereditary lordship bound to the Circle of the Nine Rivers — an ancient druidic order that serves as both spiritual authority and grim inquisition
> **Population:** 187,000

## Racial Composition
~65% Siortharan Humans, 15% Iljarnskarn and Arcavean Humans, 5% Fanari Elves, 5% Gnomes, 10% other lineages (including dhampir and Fae-cursed families)

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Peat, bog iron, alchemical reagents, grimoires, and funerary art

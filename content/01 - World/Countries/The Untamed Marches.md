# The Untamed Marches

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** The Wild  Lands
> **Former Name:** The Wildlands
> **Ruler:** None officially; governed only by clan law, druidic tradition, and Fae caprice
> **Government:** Unclaimed wilderness on the Rose subcontinent
> **Population:** 469,000

## Racial Composition
~45% Siortharan Humans, 25% Iljarnskarn Humans, 10% orcs and other bestial races, 5% Gnomes, 5% Fanari Elves, 10% assorted wanderers, Fae, and exiles

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Furs, amber, driftwood, stormglass, relics, and scavenged metals from ruined High Kingdom fortresses

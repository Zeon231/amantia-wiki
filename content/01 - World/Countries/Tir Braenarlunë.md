# Tir Braenarlunë

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** The Land of Moonlit Plains
> **Former Name:** Windwraith Island
> **Ruler:** Rí Tharan mac Caerwyn
> **Government:** Hereditary monarchy tempered by the Council of the Twelve Winds, an assembly of druids, clan chiefs, and ship captains
> **Population:** 225,600

## Racial Composition
70% Siortharan, 10% Iljarnskarn, 5% Faenari Elves, 5% Gnomes, 3% Fae, 2% Firbolgs, 2% Goliaths, and 4% other

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Salt, fish, whale oil, ambergris, silver ore, longships, and fine furs

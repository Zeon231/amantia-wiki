# Tir Aelyrhae

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** The Realm of Blessed Light
> **Former Name:** Isle of Skye
> **Ruler:** Rígan Elaria na Veyra
> **Government:** Hereditary monarchy under the Rígan, guided by the Council of Riversong (advisors of bards, merchants, and druids).
> **Population:** 487,500

## Racial Composition
76% Siortharan, 8% Faenari Elves, 3% Iljarnskarn, 4% Gnomes, 5% Fae, 4% other

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Amber, wool textiles, tin, silver, dried fish, perfumes, dyes, enchanted glass

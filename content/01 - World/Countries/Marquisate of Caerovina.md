# Marquisate of Caerovina

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** Land of the White Fort
> **Former Name:** Chartoix
> **Ruler:** Marquis Brenn Caerwyn
> **Government:** Feudal lordship with elective council oversight; nominally independent, though allied to the Ard Rígan of Séraevirhae through trade and blood oaths
> **Population:** 532,000

## Racial Composition
70% Siortharan, 10% Iljarnskarn, 5% Faenari Elves, 5% Vanaril Elves, 5% Gnomes, 3% Fae, 2% other

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Iron ore, timber, salt, livestock, and mead

# Arathenska Morka

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Nickname:** The Duchy of Arathens
> **Former Name:** Ostlynn
> **Government:** Hereditary duchy under Duchess Elayne Arathens, who holds both secular title and the honorary rank of Protector of the Petal Fields — a title granted by the Church but independent of the Hierarch’s direct rule
> **Population:** 498,400

## Racial Composition
~50% Iljarnskarn Humans, 35% Siortharan Humans, 5% Gnomes, 5% Fanari Elves, 5% other pilgrims, merchants, and foreign settlers

## Trade & Economy
**Exports:** Grain, wine, cattle, parchment, dyes, and sacred oils

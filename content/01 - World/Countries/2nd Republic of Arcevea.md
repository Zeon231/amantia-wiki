# 2nd Republic of Arcevea

> [!info] Nation Info
> **Former Name:** The Republic of Arcadia
> **Government:** Two Consules elected by democratic vote: the Head of State and Commandeer-in-Chief (each can veto the other); Senate  of Elected Senators from each Province (2 from each province) that legislate laws, regulations, and edicts; Judicial Power in the Archon Eponymos, elected by the citizens with council of 10 Archons, elected from each province
> **Population:** 1,606,700

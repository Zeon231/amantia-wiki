## Description
A small, forgotten village on the edge of something wilder — old forests, dark hills, or borderlands where old magic has deep roots. The kind of place people in cities don't think about. Ashveil doesn't appear on most maps, and those that do mark it only as a dot with no label. The surrounding land feels older than the village itself.

Currently afflicted by a curse laid by **Maren Voss** — a wandering scholar who was a hag in disguise. The curse is slow and ongoing. Residents are dying.

## Notable Districts / Areas
- The village proper — small cluster of homes, a healer's space, communal gathering area
- The edge — where the wilder land begins; where Maren Voss was sometimes seen

## History
Ashveil's history before Maren Voss's arrival is unremarkable — a borderlands village that survived by staying small and unnoticed. Maren arrived presenting herself as a wandering scholar or hedge mage. She took **Vesper Crowe** under her wing, trained him, and at some point performed a ritual that transformed him into a Hexblood. When the curse hit the village, Maren was gone. Vesper traced the signs back to her and left to find a cure.

## Secrets & Rumours
- The curse is slow — some villagers are further along than others. Corwen is still alive.
- Why Ashveil? Was the village the target, or was Vesper — and the village collateral?
- The nature of the curse (poison vs. dark magic) is still being determined

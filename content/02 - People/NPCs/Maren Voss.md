> [!warning] DM Only
> "Maren Voss" is the name she used in [[Ashveil]]. It is not her true name. Her true form and true name are unknown — to be determined by the DM.

## Appearance
**Disguise:** Presented as a middle-aged wandering scholar or hedge mage. Forgettable in exactly the right way — the kind of person a village would trust because there was nothing obviously alarming about her. Practical traveling clothes, carried books or materials consistent with a scholar's trade.

**True form:** Unknown. Has not been seen in true form by any current PC.

## Personality
Patient. Deliberate. Chose Vesper specifically and invested significant time building his trust and shaping his capabilities before acting. Knows how to read people — particularly soldiers: the loyalty, the need for a mission, the deference to authority. Used all of it. Her motives are not yet clear, which makes her more dangerous than a simple villain.

She has not gone silent since leaving Ashveil. The Witch's Mark she left in Vesper allows her to reach him through dreams. She still contacts him. She still wants something from what she made.

## Background
Maren Voss arrived in [[Ashveil]] presenting herself as a wandering scholar or hedge mage with knowledge of old magic and dark places. She took [[Vesper Crowe]] under her wing — sharpened his existing military skills, taught him to operate in spaces the army never trained him for, and earned his complete trust. At some point she performed a ritual that transformed Vesper into a Hexblood. The transformation may have been framed as a gift, a tactical advantage, or something Vesper agreed to without fully understanding.

She then cursed Ashveil and left before Vesper could confront her. By the time he traced the signs back to her, she was gone.

# Corwen

> [!info] At a Glance

## Appearance
Elderly. Weathered in the way of someone who has spent a long life in a borderlands village — not frail, just worn. The kind of person a place like Ashveil builds over generations. The curse has aged her further. She was still alive the last time Vesper sent word.

## Personality
Steady. The kind of person who sees something unsettling — like a young man's hair going silver overnight and one eye starting to shift — and decides it doesn't change who he is. She didn't understand what the hexblood transformation meant. She said it didn't matter. That's the thing Vesper carries.

## Background
Corwen is an elder and healer figure in [[Ashveil]]. She was one of the few people in the village who accepted [[Vesper Crowe]] after Maren's ritual changed him — when others pulled back or whispered, Corwen didn't flinch. She became the anchor: the face Vesper holds in mind when the road gets hard.

She is now afflicted by the curse [[Maren Voss]] laid on Ashveil. She was still alive when Vesper left. Vesper sends word when he can. Every letter without a response tightens something in his chest.

## Motivations & Goals
- Survive long enough for Vesper to find the cure
- She may have information about Maren's time in Ashveil that Vesper doesn't — she was present, she watched, and she's had time to remember things

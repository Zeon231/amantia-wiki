## Background
Aldric is [[Vesper Crowe]]'s father. He and his wife [[Nessa Crowe]] remain in [[Ashveil]], afflicted by the curse laid by [[Maren Voss]]. The hexblood mark is not in the family bloodline — it came entirely from Maren's ritual. Aldric had no part in what was done to Vesper and no knowledge of what Maren truly was.

Vesper's relationship with his parents is complicated: love, guilt, and the particular weight of having brought Maren into the village — someone who ultimately destroyed it.

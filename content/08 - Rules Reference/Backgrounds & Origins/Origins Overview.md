# Character Origins in Amantia

Origins represent where your character came from and how they became an adventurer. In Amantia, backgrounds and feats are where your origin story **leaves fingerprints on the rules** — the oath you swore in a candlelit chapel, the streetwise instincts from a port city, the stubborn resilience earned on a winter road.

---

## Background Components

Each background provides:
- **Ability Score increases** (one +2 and one +1, or three +1s — max 20)
- **An Origin Feat**
- **Proficiency in two skills**

---

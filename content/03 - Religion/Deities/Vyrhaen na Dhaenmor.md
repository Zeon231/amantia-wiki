# Vyrhaen na Dhaenmor

> [!info] Divine Info
> **Pantheon:** Siórtharan
> **Type:** Intermediate
> **Portfolio:** Necromancy, Liches, Undeath
> **Homeplane:** The Ossuary of Endless Night– A vast necropolis of black stone and bone stretching across the horizon of the Shadowfell. Its streets are lined with mausoleums whose doors open into realms of frozen time, each containing armies of loyal undead. At its heart rises the Hollow Spire, a spiraling tower of fused bone and obsidian where Vyrhaen’s throne rests beneath a great black phylactery suspended in chains of shadowsteel.

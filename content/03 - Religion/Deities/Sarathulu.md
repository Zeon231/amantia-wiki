# Sarathulu

> [!info] Divine Info
> **Pantheon:** Bakassari
> **Type:** Intermediate
> **Portfolio:** Arrogant Nobility, Espionage, Assassins, Intrigue
> **Homeplane:** The Gilded Labyrinth – A sprawling palace-city in Gehenna built entirely of veined marble, gold, and obsidian. Its walls are lined with concealed corridors, murder holes, and hidden chambers, allowing assassins and spies to pass unseen. Each hall echoes with whispers of blackmail and political intrigue, and masked courtiers dance in endless masquerades where each step is a calculated move in an eternal game of life and death. Sarathulu’s throne sits high above the Grand Masquerade Hall, shrouded in veils and shadows, from which he watches all with a smile that never reaches his eyes.

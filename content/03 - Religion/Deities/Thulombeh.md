# Thulombeh

> [!info] Divine Info
> **Pantheon:** Bakassari
> **Alignment:** N

> **Domains:** Tempest, Trickery, War
> **Portfolio:** War, Fire
> **Homeplane:** The Citadel of the Burning Banner – A colossal fortress of white-gold stone and blazing braziers, suspended above a sea of roiling clouds in the eternal midday light of Mount Celestia’s higher reaches.

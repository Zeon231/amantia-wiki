# Deity Index

Amantia's divine cosmos spans four tiers and fourteen cultural pantheons. Click any name to open that deity's note.

> [!info] Divine Hierarchy
> **Elder Elemental Gods** → ancient, alien, worshipped by monsters and cults
> **Primordial Gods** → elemental powers; worshipped across cultures
> **Racial / Cultural Pantheons** → the everyday gods of mortal civilisations
> **Demigods** → Giant, Dragon, Fae, Bestial powers; regional or species-specific
